Our Community

Our beautiful workspaces are nothing without the thriving community of 200+ creatives that call them home.

Since opening the doors to our first Gather Round, building a community of creatives has been at the heart of our mission. We’re passionate about bringing together collectives of diverse creative people that together, champion a culture of openness, inclusivity and solidarity.

What makes us different

The Hub was established in 2018 as one of the projects of DaFED, a non-profit organisation devoted to networking and organising educational events for IT community.

Our mission is to create a supportive and warm environment where companies and individuals can connect and collaborate. The space where everyone can work, share ideas, initiate some activities and create meaningful bonds.

Hubitat is situated in the building with a historical significance called Vatican. It was built in 1930. and commissioned by Roman Catholic church community. The architect Daka Popović emphasized the structure of this palace by putting the eclectic decor on the facade. You can even notice some flat ionic columns on the facade. There are a variety of restaurants and pubs nearby.
